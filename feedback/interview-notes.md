# User Interview — SchoolHub

- **Who:** Aye Aye (student, 16 years old, my cousin)
- **When:** 2026-06-26
- **How:** In person — ~15 min

## What they do today (without your project)

Aye Aye checks 3 different Telegram groups for school announcements, asks friends if she missed anything, and keeps a photo of the notice board for her class schedule. She's missed quizzes because announcements got buried in chat.

## What they liked

- "Everything is in one place — I don't need to switch between apps."
- "The curriculum page is helpful — I can see what subjects I'll have next year."
- "I like that it works in Myanmar language. My English is okay but Burmese is easier."
- "The purple design looks fun, not boring like other school apps."

## What confused them / what's missing

- "I couldn't tell which announcements were new since my last visit."
- "I want to see only my class announcements, not all grades."
- "No way to comment or react to announcements."
- "I wish there was a timetable view for my daily schedule."
- "The register form is too long — just let me use my phone number."

## What would make them actually use it

- "If it showed new announcements with a badge or highlight."
- "If my real teachers posted announcements on it daily."
- "If I could see my personal class schedule, not just the class list."

## What I'll change (next steps)

- [ ] Add "new" badge on announcements posted in last 24 hours
- [ ] Filter announcements by grade/class
- [ ] Add weekly timetable view for enrolled classes
- [ ] Simplify registration (add phone number option)
